Welcome to polar-flow-server

Self-hosted health analytics for your Polar devices

Get Started

Step 1: Configure Polar OAuth App

To sync your Polar data, you need to create an OAuth app:

  1. Go to admin.polaraccesslink.com
  2. Create a new client/app
  3. Set redirect URI to: {{ callback_url }}
  4. Copy your Client ID and Client Secret below

Step 2: Connect Your Account

After saving your OAuth credentials, you'll be able to connect your Polar account and start syncing data.

What gets synced:

  • Sleep data (scores, stages, HRV, heart rate)
  • Nightly Recharge (ANS charge, recovery metrics)
  • Daily activity (steps, calories, distance)
  • Exercises (workouts with detailed metrics)
